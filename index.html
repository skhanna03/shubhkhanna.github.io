I'm an undergrad & builder at Stanford working on AI algorithms modeled after the way the brain works.

Previously, I've built Ocular Diagnostics, a VC-backed brain health startup. We used eye movements to detect the onset of neurobehavioral disorders.

In my time at Stanford, I've built a boba vending machine, a headset that uses brain signals to steer a wheelchair, and a ton of LLM apps. Reach out to me if you want to grab coffee, talk about the limits of perception, or just build something cool.
